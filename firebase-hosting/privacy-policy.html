Privacy Policy for Zifty

Effective Date: 19th October 2024

1. Information Collected

Zifty does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or other identifiable details.

2. No Tracking of User Activity

Zifty does not gather user activity or behavior. Zifty does not store search queries, mouse-clicks, or any other user interactions.

3. Data Sharing

Your information is used exclusively for the operation of the Zifty extension. Zifty transfers your search queries to providers of secondhand goods listings and/or to a third party AI completion engine to pre-process search queries when required. No personal information is shared with these.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If significant changes are made, you will be notified through the extension or other appropriate means. The date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of this document.
