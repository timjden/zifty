Privacy Policy for Zifty

Effective Date: 9th September 2024

1. Information We Collect

Zifty does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or other identifiable details. The only data we store is:

  • Subscription Status: Whether you are a free or paid subscriber, including subscription dates.
  • User Preferences: Settings within the extension, such as which toggles or features you have enabled.

If you choose not to sign in with Google, we do not store any user data at all.

2. No Tracking of User Activity

Zifty does not gather user activity or behavior. We do not store search queries. All search queries processed through Zifty are handled client-side, and no search data is sent or stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the Zifty extension:

  • Subscription Status: To ensure that you have access to the appropriate features based on your subscription.
  • User Preferences: To remember and apply your preferred settings within the extension.

4. Data Sharing

Zifty does not sell, trade, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the operation of the Zifty extension.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet-based service can be fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the extension or other appropriate means. The date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of this document.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at timjden@gmail.com.
